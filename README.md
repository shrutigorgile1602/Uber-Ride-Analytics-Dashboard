# 🚖 Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Uber ride data and generate actionable business insights on booking trends, cancellations, revenue, and customer satisfaction.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Ride-sharing platforms generate massive amounts of data daily. This project aims to analyze Uber ride data to:

- Identify booking patterns and demand trends
- Understand cancellation behavior
- Analyze revenue distribution
- Improve customer and driver experience

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Track overall ride performance  
- Analyze cancellations and their root causes  
- Identify peak demand periods  
- Evaluate revenue streams  
- Provide data-driven business recommendations  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

- Total Bookings: 148K+  
- Success Rate: 65.9%  
- Cancellation Rate: 25%  
- Customer Rating: 4.4  
- Driver Rating: 4.2  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### Overall Performance
- Ride volume trends over time  
- Booking status distribution  

### Revenue Analysis
- Revenue by payment method  
- UPI as the highest contributor  
- Top customers by booking value  

### Cancellation Analysis
- Customer vs Driver cancellations  
- Major reasons:
  - Wrong address  
  - Change of plans  
  - Driver-related issues  

### Demand Analysis
- Peak demand during morning & evening hours  
- Day vs Hour heatmap for ride activity  

### Advanced Analytics
- Funnel analysis (Booking → Completion)  
- Scatter plot (Distance vs Booking Value)  
- Location-based demand analysis  

---

---

## 📊 Key Visualizations

- Time Series Chart

- Map Visualization

- Scatter Plot  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- High 25% cancellation rate indicates operational inefficiencies  
- Customer cancellations are higher than driver cancellations  
- UPI dominates digital payment methods  
- Ride demand peaks during office hours  
- Strong relationship between distance and booking value  

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Improve GPS accuracy to reduce wrong address issues  
- Provide incentives to drivers to reduce cancellations  
- Implement surge pricing during peak hours  
- Improve app performance to reduce technical issues  
- Focus on high-demand locations for better driver allocation
